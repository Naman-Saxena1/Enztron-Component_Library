Card

Cards are content containers that describe a specific topic/item and provide relevant options of action to the user.
Enztron currently has 6 types of card components -
1) Cards with Badges
2) Cards with dismiss
3) Cards with text overlay
4) Text only cards
5) Vertical/Horizontal cards
6) Cards with shadow
